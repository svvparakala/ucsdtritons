UCSD Tritons

Triton Gaming's collegiate League of Legends team

>

"Work hard, play harder."

That's the motto by which all principle members of Triton Gaming live by. Thereby, as competitive teams are one of the subdivisions of the organization, we too follow this code. Before Triton Gaming was established, the predominant gaming group on campus was League of Tritons. Riot Games had created the world's most popular video game and it was all the rage across college campuses nationwide. Why? Because this was the time we got to show our fierce competitive nature. The drive and motivation to be amongst the top players and the constant urge for self improvement, catapulted young gamers to high levels of competition. Many of these players were college students. And it just so happens UC San Diego was home to a couple of them. Before you could blink colleges across the nation had assembled high caliber teams, fresh with talented players eager to showcase their skills.

Coaching UCSD's collegiate League team has taught me more than I could have ever imagined, and most importantly it brought me close to understanding what affects competitive performance. A single player cannot face down five enemies without four allies of his own. The players cannot improve themselves without the help of their coaches. The team cannot operate without the support of their organization. Finally, it is the pride of wearing that logo on their jacket and the motivation to leave their mark on a legacy that pushes a team forward. That is the team I have coached each time for the past two years. A team that is backed by the love and support of Triton Gaming and it's amazing officers. A team that will never back down from even the fiercest rivals. A team that is striving to prove to the rest of the nation just how fearsome a Triton is.


- Lens (Head Coach, Triton Gaming)
